We’re business-savvy technologists

While we definitely get excited about new technologies, our focus is on building solutions that improve service quality and security but more importantly, help your business. In working with clients, we seek to first understand your business and what’ important to you and your customers. Once we understand your vision and objectives clearly, we get to work supporting you with the right technical expertise and resources. When troubleshooting specific problems for clients, we look at the workflow and processes and then research thoroughly to find and fix the root causes of problems.

Our flexibility saves you time and money

Loginsoft’s iterative development process means you see demonstrable deliverables quickly. With this approach, you can make changes earlier, reducing risk and ensuring effectiveness. As a boutique firm, our senior team members stay actively involved in managing teams and working directly with you. These leaders select the right talent from our deep bench of technical experts and partners to support short or long-term needs whether small or large projects.

We deliver low total cost of ownership

To ensure right-sized solutions for your business we often leverage existing components and select only proven, best-of-breed technologies. Where appropriate, we will integrate applications with your legacy systems through open source programming, JAVA, XML, or Web Services. This intelligent integration, combined with practical development, helps simplify maintenance and reduces the total cost of ownership.

We’re people-savvy technologists

Successful technology solutions start with giving people a voice and sense of ownership in the initiative. At Loginsoft, we solicit input early and often to keep the end user top of mind when matching technology and solutions to the user community. Because we know that user involvement results in faster adoption, lower training time, and happier users. After all, what’s the point of great technology if people struggle using it? We are proud that our solutions have high implementation success. And, as much as we like shiny, new, cutting-edge technology, we know that sometimes “low-tech” is the right choice. We’re not afraid to suggest a simple solution when it’s the most cost-effective and wisest option.
